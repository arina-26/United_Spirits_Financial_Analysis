# 📊 Financial Analysis of United Spirits Ltd. (Excel Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive financial analysis of **United Spirits Ltd.** using Microsoft Excel. The analysis is based on consolidated income statements and common-size financial data across multiple years.

The goal is to evaluate the company’s financial performance, cost structure, and profitability trends through structured data analysis and visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue trends over multiple financial years
* Evaluate cost structure (COGS, OPEX, Excise Duty impact)
* Perform common-size analysis for better comparability
* Study profitability metrics such as Gross Profit, EBIT, and Net Profit
* Derive insights for financial decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Financial Statement Analysis
* Common-Size Analysis
* Pivot Tables & Charts
* KPI Calculation

## 📂 Dataset

* Company: United Spirits Ltd.
* Data Type: Consolidated Income Statement
* Period Covered: FY2022 – FY2025

---

## 📊 Key Financial Metrics

* Total Revenue / Income
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
* Gross Profit
* Operating Expenses (OPEX)
* EBIT (Earnings Before Interest & Tax)
* Profit Before Tax (PBT)
* Net Profit (PAT)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Revenue shows fluctuations, with a decline from FY2022 to FY2023 followed by recovery in later years
* COGS remains a major component (~79–86%), significantly impacting profitability
* Gross Profit margin improved from ~13.5% (2022) to ~20.6% (2025) 
* Operating Profit (EBIT) margin increased steadily, indicating improved operational efficiency
* Net Profit margin improved from ~2.76% (2022) to ~5.73% (2025) 
* Excise duty constitutes a significant portion of total costs, heavily influencing margins
